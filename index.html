About

Waseem Jan is Full Stack Software Engineer at BriteCore. Previously Waseem co-founded Chef Galaxy (Social marketplace for the dishes and culinary services). Though Chef Galaxy couldn't get successful but as a sole engineer in his team, it gave him tremendous exposure to the whole life cycle of the project right from the first line of the code to the final production deployment. As the budget was tight, he did everything on his own without any DBA, DevOPs, Testing or UI guys.

In the past, Waseem has assumed the roles of DevOps and Frontend Engineer too.

Currently he is living in a small and peaceful town of Kamra, Pakistan with his loving wife Yusaira and two sons Azlan and Zaman. He is also a devoted lover of parrots and maintains his own aviary having dozens of cockatiels and budgies.

Waseem holds a Bachelors in Software Engineering from the GIK Institute.
