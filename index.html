About

Waseem Jan is a Full Stack Software Engineer at BriteCore. Previously, Waseem co-founded Chef Galaxy, a social marketplace for dishes and culinary services. Although Chef Galaxy couldn't become successful, as the sole engineer in his team, it gave him a tremendous opportunity to learn and grow.

In the past, Waseem has assumed the roles of DevOps and Frontend Engineer as well.

Currently, he is living in the small and peaceful town of Kamra, Pakistan with his loving wife Yusaira and two sons, Azlan and Zaman. He is also a devoted lover of parrots and maintains his own aviary having dozens of cockatiels and budgies.

Waseem holds a bachelor's degree in Software Engineering from the GIK Institute.
